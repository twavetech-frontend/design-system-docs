# Loading Indicators

Loading Indicator는 비동기 작업의 진행 상태를 시각적으로 표현하는 컴포넌트입니다. 3가지 스타일과 4가지 사이즈를 제공합니다.

Figma 컴포넌트: `Loading indicator`

---

## Style

### Line simple

회색 트랙 위에 보라색 호가 회전하는 가장 단순한 형태입니다.

<ComponentPreview code={`<LoadingIndicator style="line-simple" size="sm" />
<LoadingIndicator style="line-simple" size="md" />
<LoadingIndicator style="line-simple" size="lg" />
<LoadingIndicator style="line-simple" size="xl" />`}>
  <div style={{ display: 'flex', gap: 48, alignItems: 'flex-end' }}>
    <LoadingIndicator style="line-simple" size="sm" />
    <LoadingIndicator style="line-simple" size="md" />
    <LoadingIndicator style="line-simple" size="lg" />
    <LoadingIndicator style="line-simple" size="xl" />
  </div>
</ComponentPreview>

### Line spinner

트랙 없이 그라데이션 호만 회전하는 형태입니다.

<ComponentPreview code={`<LoadingIndicator style="line-spinner" size="sm" />
<LoadingIndicator style="line-spinner" size="md" />
<LoadingIndicator style="line-spinner" size="lg" />
<LoadingIndicator style="line-spinner" size="xl" />`}>
  <div style={{ display: 'flex', gap: 48, alignItems: 'flex-end' }}>
    <LoadingIndicator style="line-spinner" size="sm" />
    <LoadingIndicator style="line-spinner" size="md" />
    <LoadingIndicator style="line-spinner" size="lg" />
    <LoadingIndicator style="line-spinner" size="xl" />
  </div>
</ComponentPreview>

### Dot circle

12개의 점이 원형으로 배치되어 회전하는 형태입니다. 점의 투명도가 점진적으로 변해 회전 방향을 강조합니다.

<ComponentPreview code={`<LoadingIndicator style="dot-circle" size="sm" />
<LoadingIndicator style="dot-circle" size="md" />
<LoadingIndicator style="dot-circle" size="lg" />
<LoadingIndicator style="dot-circle" size="xl" />`}>
  <div style={{ display: 'flex', gap: 48, alignItems: 'flex-end' }}>
    <LoadingIndicator style="dot-circle" size="sm" />
    <LoadingIndicator style="dot-circle" size="md" />
    <LoadingIndicator style="dot-circle" size="lg" />
    <LoadingIndicator style="dot-circle" size="xl" />
  </div>
</ComponentPreview>

## Without supporting text

`supportingText` prop을 비우면 라벨 없이 스피너만 표시됩니다.

<ComponentPreview code={`<LoadingIndicator style="line-simple" size="md" supportingText="" />
<LoadingIndicator style="line-spinner" size="md" supportingText="" />
<LoadingIndicator style="dot-circle" size="md" supportingText="" />`}>
  <div style={{ display: 'flex', gap: 32, alignItems: 'center' }}>
    <LoadingIndicator style="line-simple" size="md" supportingText="" />
    <LoadingIndicator style="line-spinner" size="md" supportingText="" />
    <LoadingIndicator style="dot-circle" size="md" supportingText="" />
  </div>
</ComponentPreview>

## Custom label

`supportingText`로 라벨을 자유롭게 변경할 수 있습니다.

<ComponentPreview code={`<LoadingIndicator style="line-spinner" size="md" supportingText="Uploading..." />
<LoadingIndicator style="line-spinner" size="md" supportingText="Saving changes" />
<LoadingIndicator style="line-spinner" size="md" supportingText="잠시만 기다려주세요" />`}>
  <div style={{ display: 'flex', gap: 48, alignItems: 'flex-end' }}>
    <LoadingIndicator style="line-spinner" size="md" supportingText="Uploading..." />
    <LoadingIndicator style="line-spinner" size="md" supportingText="Saving changes" />
    <LoadingIndicator style="line-spinner" size="md" supportingText="잠시만 기다려주세요" />
  </div>
</ComponentPreview>
